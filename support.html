Neck Sense Support

Your posture monitoring companion for AirPods

Getting Started

Neck Sense helps you maintain healthy posture by monitoring your head position using AirPods Pro, AirPods Max, or AirPods (3rd generation) motion sensors.

Requirements

  • AirPods Pro, AirPods Max, or AirPods (3rd generation)
  • iOS 14.0 or later / macOS 10.15 or later (Mac Catalyst)
  • Bluetooth connection

Frequently Asked Questions

Why isn't my app detecting my AirPods?

Make sure your AirPods are:

  • Connected to your device via Bluetooth
  • In your ears (not in the case)
  • One of the supported models (Pro, Max, or 3rd generation)

Try disconnecting and reconnecting your AirPods, or restarting the app.

Which AirPods models are supported?

Neck Sense works with AirPods that have motion sensors:

  • AirPods Pro (1st and 2nd generation)
  • AirPods Max
  • AirPods (3rd generation)

Earlier AirPods models (1st and 2nd generation) do not have motion sensors and are not supported.

How do I adjust the posture threshold?

Premium users can customize the threshold:

  1. Open Settings (gear icon)
  2. Adjust "Threshold Angle" slider (15-40°)
  3. Adjust "Alert Interval" (5-40 seconds)

Free tier users have fixed settings: 25° threshold and 15-second interval.

What's the difference between Free and Premium?

Free Tier:

  • 3 sessions per day
  • 30 minutes per session
  • Fixed settings (25° threshold, 15s interval)

Premium Tier:

  • Unlimited daily sessions
  • Unlimited session duration
  • Customizable threshold (15-40°)
  • Customizable alert interval (5-40s)
  • Priority support
How do I restore my purchase on a new device?

Your premium subscription is tied to your Apple ID:

  1. Install Neck Sense on your new device
  2. Open the app and go to Settings
  3. Tap "Upgrade to Premium"
  4. Tap "Restore Purchases"

Your subscription will be restored automatically if you're signed in with the same Apple ID.

The app says "AirPods Idle" - what does this mean?

This means your AirPods are connected but not actively sending motion data. Common causes:

  • AirPods are not in your ears
  • AirPods have entered sleep mode
  • Motion sensors need to wake up

Solution: Make sure AirPods are properly in your ears. The status should change to "Tracking Active" within a few seconds.

Does the app drain my AirPods battery faster?

The app uses minimal power. The motion sensors in your AirPods are already active during normal use. Neck Sense simply reads the data that's already being generated. Battery impact is negligible compared to music playback.

Can I use the app on multiple devices?

Yes! Premium purchases are tied to your Apple ID, so you can use Neck Sense on all your devices (iPhone, iPad, Mac) with the same subscription.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through Apple:

  1. Open Settings app on your iPhone
  2. Tap your name at the top
  3. Tap "Subscriptions"
  4. Select "Neck Sense"
  5. Tap "Cancel Subscription"

On Mac: App Store → Account → Manage Subscriptions

Troubleshooting

App not detecting my posture

  • Ensure AirPods are in your ears, not in the case
  • Check that motion tracking status shows "Tracking Active"
  • Try removing and reinserting your AirPods
  • Restart the app

Alerts not appearing

  • Check that notifications are enabled for Neck Sense in iOS Settings
  • Ensure you have bad posture for longer than the alert interval (default: 15 seconds)
  • Check your notification settings (Do Not Disturb might be enabled)

App crashes or freezes

  • Force quit the app and reopen
  • Restart your device
  • Check for app updates in the App Store
  • Reinstall the app if problems persist

Privacy & Data

Neck Sense processes all motion data locally on your device. We do not collect or store your posture data on our servers. The only data we collect is:

  • Purchase information (via Apple and Adapty) for subscription management
  • Crash reports (anonymous, if you opt in)
